DEPARTMENT OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

With this app, rural education is revolutionized by providing a comprehensive platform for managing educational resources, fostering motivation, and increasing awareness of government schemes. Module details are as follows:

  1. Role-Based Login
    The app provides secure, role-specific access for students and teachers.
    • Students: Can view personal goals, study materials, and achievements.
    • Teachers: Can manage attendance, upload resources, and track student progress.
  2. Study Material
    Teachers can upload PDFs, images, and videos, enabling students to access educational resources anytime, enhancing their learning experience.
  3. Motivational Quotes
    A curated collection of motivational quotes is displayed to inspire students and promote a positive learning mindset.
  4. Yojanas
    This module enables users to explore educational government schemes and initiatives, helping rural students and families access opportunities.
  5. Achievements
    Teachers can post and share student accomplishments, fostering pride and recognition in the community.
  6. Attendance Management
    The app features an efficient attendance system with automated date and time records, simplifying attendance tracking for teachers.
  7. Achievements
    Teachers can post updates about school events, deadlines, and notices, ensuring students and parents are always informed.

Description of Project With this project, users can streamline the process of seed sowing with enhanced efficiency and precision. Module details are as follows:

  1. Automated Seed Sowing
    The bot sows seeds systematically, ensuring optimal spacing and depth for better crop growth.
  2. Remote Control
    Users can control the bot remotely using a dedicated app or interface for field operations.
  3. Navigation and Mapping
    The bot uses sensors and mapping technology to navigate fields and cover designated areas.
  4. Adjustable Seed Parameters
    Parameters like seed type, sowing rate, and depth can be adjusted according to user requirements.
  5. Real-time Monitoring
    Provides real-time updates on the sowing process, including areas covered and time remaining.
