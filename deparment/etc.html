Smart Solar Street Lamp

This project enhances rural lighting infrastructure by integrating solar energy with intelligent control mechanisms. It aims to provide sustainable, cost-effective lighting solutions for villages, improving safety and socio-economic development. Module details are as follows:

  1. Solar Energy Integration
    Harnesses solar panels to power the street lamps, ensuring eco-friendly and sustainable operation.
  2. Automatic Night Light
    The lamp automatically turns on at night, providing consistent illumination without manual intervention.
  3. Anti-Theft Alarm
    Equipped with a security alarm system to prevent theft of the lamp components.
  4. Automatic Cleaning System
    Features a sprinkler and motorized cleaning mechanism that operates every three days, maintaining panel efficiency and increasing energy output by 15-25%.
  5. Battery Management
    Includes an automatic battery cutoff system to protect against low voltage, ensuring long battery life and reliability.
  6. Cost-Efficient Design
    The system is designed for affordability, with components like a solar panel, pump, charge controller, and water tank costing approximately â‚¹13,000 in total.

Agricultural Drone

A drone-based solution designed for automated urea spraying in farmlands, optimizing farming operations through precision technology.

  1. Automated Spraying System
    The drone sprays fertilizers or pesticides evenly across the field, reducing manual labor.
  2. GPS-Based Navigation
    Uses GPS mapping to ensure precise coverage of agricultural land.
  3. Obstacle Detection System
    Equipped with radar-based obstacle detection to prevent collisions and ensure safe operation.
  4. Remote Control Operation
    Farmers can control the drone via a mobile app, reducing the need for direct fieldwork.
  5. Battery-Efficient Design
    Rechargeable batteries with a quick charging mechanism allow continuous operation with minimal downtime.

E-Bicycle Project

A cost-effective electric bicycle designed for rural mobility, offering an eco-friendly and efficient transportation solution.

  1. Electric-Powered Mobility
    Retrofitted with an electric motor to assist in pedaling, reducing physical effort.
  2. Rechargeable Battery System
    Powered by a Lithium-ion battery for long-distance travel on a single charge.
  3. Dual Mode Operation
    Can function as both a manual bicycle and an electric-powered vehicle.
  4. Affordable and Sustainable
    Conversion of regular bicycles into e-bikes at 40-50% lower cost than a new electric bicycle.
  5. User-Friendly Design
    Lightweight and easy to maintain, ensuring long-term usability for rural populations.

Security Systems

A project focused on enhancing rural security using modern surveillance and automation technologies.

  1. CCTV Installation
    Cameras installed in key village locations to monitor activities and prevent theft or vandalism.
  2. Smart Irrigation System
    Automated water control using sensors to optimize agricultural irrigation.
  3. Solar-Powered Electronic Weighing Machine
    A weighing system powered by solar energy, reducing electricity dependency.
  4. Automatic Bell System for Schools
    A school bell that rings automatically at scheduled intervals, eliminating manual operation.

Solar-Powered Smart Composter

A cost-effective, eco-friendly solution for managing food waste using IoT and solar power, designed to enhance composting efficiency for rural and urban use.

  1. Automated Composting
    Integrates IoT sensors to monitor temperature, pH, and moisture levels in real-time.
  2. Renewable Energy Source
    Powered by solar panels, ensuring off-grid functionality.
  3. Manual Override
    Allows for both automated and manual control of composting parameters.
  4. Resource Optimization
    Uses renewable energy and real-time data collection for efficient composting.
  5. Smart Monitoring
    Real-time alerts and data tracking for ease of use.
  6. Low Maintenance
    Sustainable design with minimal manual intervention.
