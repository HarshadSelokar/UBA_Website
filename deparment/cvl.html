DEPARTMENT OF CIVIL ENGINEERING

Anandwan's Mapping and Architectural Model Creation, we aim to create accurate and detailed 3D models of existing structures and develop layered maps showcasing the growth of Anandwan village from 1949 to the present. Module details are as follows:

  1. Site Analysis & Data Collection
    A comprehensive survey of Anandwan village will be conducted to gather data about housing settlements, typologies, and material technologies of structures.
  2. Historical Growth Mapping
    Depict the village's development over different decades through layered maps highlighting changes in architecture, age of buildings, and spatial distribution.
  3. 3D Architectural Modeling
    Develop realistic, interactive 3D models of the villageâ€™s buildings, complete with textures and dimensions for a detailed visual representation.
  4. Data Processing & AutoCAD Maps
    Process the collected data to create accurate 2D floor plans and elevation drawings using AutoCAD software.
  5. Final Model Presentation
    Assemble all maps and 3D models into a comprehensive report with technical specifications and methodologies, culminating in a final presentation for stakeholders.
