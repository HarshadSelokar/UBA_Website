DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER ENGINEERING

Description of Project With this Project (application development) users may access virtual medical consultations from anywhere, at any time. Module details are as follows:

  1. Appointment Booking
    Villagers (Patients) can book appointments online for any doctors or any diseases at a Convenient time slots.
  2. Search Doctors
    (Villagers)Patients can search the doctor by doctor name,availability, specialties, or symptoms.
  3. Notifications
    (Villagers)patient will get notifications for upcoming appointments, rescheduled appointments, shared medicine orders, and follow-up appointments.
  4. Upload Health Documents
    (Villagers) Patients can upload documents such as medicine orders and other health-related documents to the app.
  5. In-app Payment
    Villagers (Patients) can pay doctor's fees in-app payment feature at the time of booking itself.

The Pharmacy Module streamlines access to medicines in rural areas by connecting patients with local pharmacies. It simplifies ordering and prescription management, ensuring timely delivery of essential medications, addressing the lack of healthcare infrastructure in villages.

  1. Medicine Selection
    Villagers (Patients) can browse or search for medicines by name or category through the app.
  2. User Registration
    Villagers (Patients) can register as users, while pharmacies can register in admin mode for managing orders and stock.
  3. Secure Login
    Villagers (Patients) can log in using biometric authentication or Google verification for secure access.
  4. Profile Management
    Villagers (Patients) can manage personal details, track order history, and update contact information.
  5. Data Management
    Admins (Pharmacies) can manage medicine details such as name, price, description, and images, with data securely stored using Firebase Database.

The Patient Module provides villagers with easy access to healthcare services by enabling them to book appointments, consult doctors online, and manage their health records from the app. This module bridges the gap between rural communities and medical professionals, ensuring timely healthcare without the need for physical travel.

  1. Appointment Booking
    Villagers (Patients) can browse or search for medicines by name or category through the app.Villagers (Patients) can book appointments online with doctors at convenient time slots.
  2. User Registration
    Villagers (Patients) can register and create personal profiles to manage their health records and appointments.
  3. Secure Login
    Villagers (Patients) can log in using biometric authentication or Google verification for easy and secure access.
  4. Notifications
    Villagers (Patients) receive notifications for upcoming appointments, rescheduled consultations, and follow-up reminders.
  5. Profile Management
    Villagers (Patients) can manage personal details, view appointment history, and upload health-related documents.
