OUR VISITS
January 17, 2020
Visit to Panjari Lodhi village

Description

  • Engaged with villagers to understand local issues, including lack of medical facilities and infrastructure.

  • Identified the need for health camps and improved waste management.

  • Discussed installing solar street lamps and enhancing school facilities.

  • Strengthened community relations and gathered insights for future development projects.
November 20, 2021
Visit to Villages

Description

  • Team of faculty and students visited villages to engage with village officials and discuss rural issues.

  • Interaction with Sarpanch and Gramsevak.

  • Discussions on waste management, Anganwadi infrastructure, and water purification.

December 30, 2022
Mahatma Gandhi Institute for Rural Industrialization, Wardha

Description

  • Explored Mahatma Gandhi’s rural development initiatives and sustainable technologies.

  • Engaged in discussions on rural industrialization at MGIRI, Wardha.

  • Gained insights into Gandhi’s contributions to village industries and self-sustainability.

  • Enhanced awareness of practical solutions for rural upliftment and innovation.
February 1, 2023
Visit to Khatmari and Nearby Villages

Description

  • Engaged with villagers to identify issues like waste disposal and lack of medical facilities.

  • Highlighted the need for health camps and solar street lamps.

  • Discussed infrastructure improvements for Anganwadi schools.

  • Strengthened community ties through direct interaction and needs assessment.
April 6, 2024
Visit to Anandwan, Warora

Description

  • Visited Anandwan to learn about rural development and community-driven initiatives.

  • Engaged with Dr. Vikas Amte to understand sustainable livelihood projects.

  • Explored programs focused on healthcare, education, and vocational training.

April 24, 2024
Drone Project Demonstration at Khatmari

Description

  • Demonstrated drone technology for automated urea spraying in Khatmari village.

  • Showcased how drones can improve farming efficiency and reduce labor.

  • Highlighted the benefits of precision agriculture and resource optimization.

  • Engaged with villagers to explain the project’s impact on local farming practices.
