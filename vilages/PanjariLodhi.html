Panjri Lo

About

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Panjari Lodhi village is 535904. Panjari Lodhi village is located in Nagpur Rural tehsil of Nagpur district in Maharashtra, India. It is situated 25km away from sub-district headquarters Nagpur (tehsildar office) and 25km away from district headquarters Nagpur. As per 2009 stats, Panjri Lo is the gram panchayat of Panjari Lodhi village. The total geographical area of the village is 322.24 hectares. Panjari Lodhi has a total population of 474 people, out of which the male population is 238 while the female population is 236. The literacy rate of Panjari Lodhi village is 78.48% out of which 86.55% of males and 70.34% of females are literate. There are about 101 houses in Panjari Lodhi village. Pincode of panjari lodhi village locality is 441108.
